I've built a couple of desks out of wood. Check 'em out!

I built this desk using 2x4s for the legs, 1x4s for the frame and leg supports, and 3/4 inch MDF for the top. It's really solid! The front legs taper in at a slight angle (a little more than 8 degrees measured from the vertical); I like the way this looks. I used a chop saw to cut all the angles, including the bevelled edges so the frame looks nice. I cut the top from a 4x8 foot piece using a cicular saw! Now I don't have to keep all my electronics on the ground!

I was so satisfied with my first desk that I wanted to build a second one at my mom's house! This one is built with identical materials, and a very similar asthetic. I even included the slight tapering angle to the outer leg. But instead of being a freestanding desk, this one is supported in the back by a scrap piece of MDF that is mounted directly onto the wall. I'm very pleased, and hopefully will have more space now to work on my projects!
