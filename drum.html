As a creative guy who loves to have custom, hand-made things, I have longed for a custom painted drum set. I had the idea a long time ago, but only after graduating college have I had the time to make it a reality. For this project I called upon my sister, artist-extraordinaire, to help me create a drum I'd love. Check it out!

One side of the drum...

And the other!

I'm so pleased with the drum now. I painted one side of it, and my sister did the other. It totally fulfills my vision, and it's awesome that I have this thing I made with her. It's painted like a cross section of the ocean, and a favorite song lyric of mine is painted on: "Loving is the heart when the self loses precedence, and I am just a wave in the sea of relentlessness" Because the waves that I painted look a bit more like sea creature fins, I asked my sister to paint on a Yilané, a reptilian creature from Harry Harrison's Eden trilogy. I imagine the fins to be those of the Uruketo, the Yilané's ocean-going vessel, and the particular Yilané to be the complentative Enge, whom holds the knowledge of the power of life and death. May her voice be heard through the thump of my bass drum!!
