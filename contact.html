About Me

I grew up in Gainesville, Florida, with my loving family and awesome friends. In high school I loved (and still do!) to run, make music, and learn. In 2013 I left home to attend Cornell University where I got a degree in Applied and Engineering Physics. I made some great friends there too, making more music in my band Shull and playing Ultimate Frisbee on the Cornell Buds. This summer I'm doing a long hike in the Olympic Peninsula of Washington state, and then coming back home to find the right thing for me to do next!
